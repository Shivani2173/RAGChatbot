Groww Gold ETF FOF Direct Growth is a Commodities Mutual Fund Scheme launched by Groww Mutual Fund. This scheme was made available to investors on 10 Apr 2008. Wilfred Peter Gonsalves is the Current Fund Manager of Groww Gold ETF FOF Direct Growth fund. The fund currently has an Asset Under Management(AUM) of ₹5,337 Cr and the Latest NAV as of 02 Jul 2026 is ₹17.48.
The Groww Gold ETF FOF Direct Growth is rated High risk. Minimum SIP Investment is set to ₹500. Minimum Lumpsum Investment is ₹500. Exit load of 1%, if redeemed within 30 days.

The scheme seeks to provide returns that are in line with returns provided by Groww Gold ETF.
