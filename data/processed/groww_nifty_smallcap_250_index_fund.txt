Groww Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Fund Direct Growth
Groww Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Fund Direct Growth is a Equity Mutual Fund Scheme launched by Groww Mutual Fund. This scheme was made available to investors on 10 Apr 2008. Aakash Ashokkumar Chauhan is the Current Fund Manager of Groww Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Fund Direct Growth fund. The fund currently has an Asset Under Management(AUM) of ₹5,548 Cr and the Latest NAV as of 14 Jul 2026 is ₹11.99.
The Groww Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Fund Direct Growth is rated Very High risk. Minimum SIP Investment is set to ₹500. Minimum Lumpsum Investment is ₹500. Exit load of 0.25%, if redeemed within 7 days.
Investment Objective
The Scheme seeks to investment return that corresponds to the performance of Nifty Smallcap 250 Total Return Index subject to tracking error.
